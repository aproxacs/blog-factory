### English

Search ad revenue hit an all-time high — because of AI.

Counterintuitive, right? At the exact moment AI was supposedly killing search, Google's Q4 2025 search ad revenue jumped 17% year-over-year to $63.1 billion. A record.

The logic is simple: ads moved from search results into AI answers. If people no longer click links and just read AI summaries, you sell ads inside those summaries. Google is already doing this — ad impressions inside AI Overviews surged 394% in a single year.

But the more fundamental shift is still ahead. When agents shop, book, and decide on our behalf, the target of advertising changes entirely. You no longer persuade the human — you persuade the agent. B2C becomes B2A: Business to Agent. The industry has already started playing this game under the name GEO (Generative Engine Optimization).

Humans are retaining only one thing: the desire for what they want. The practical intelligence — searching, allocating attention, making choices — is being delegated to AI. Capital is following that delegated power.
